## НОВЫЙ КОНТЕНТ (из ответов владельца) — FAQ и детали

### FAQ — логистика (для /school-shows, /birthdays, /booking)
- **Do we need a stage or a power outlet?** No — we bring everything, including our own sound. We just need a flat space of about 20 m² (~200 sq ft); shade if you're outdoors. No outlet required.
- **How long do you need to set up?** About 15–20 minutes before, and the same to pack up.
- **Can you perform outdoors?** Yes — on a flat, shaded, wind-free spot (our set uses a cloth backdrop). For rain or strong wind, please have an indoor backup.
- **Where can you perform?** Homes, classrooms, parks, restaurants — almost anywhere.

### FAQ — booking (для /booking)
- **How do we book and pay?** Tell us your date, place and group size. A **$100 deposit** holds the date; the balance is paid on the day. Pay by cash, Zelle or check.
- **Can we cancel or reschedule?** Cancel at least **2 days** ahead; reschedule for illness or weather with **1 day's** notice.
- **What if more children come than expected?** A small surcharge applies.

### /birthdays — добавить детали
- The birthday child dances, gets a **"Happy Birthday" song** and a special finale greeting, and a **small gift** (a soft toy and balloons). They can have a little role if they'd like. We usually **stay 10–15 minutes** afterward. **Up to ~20 children** is typical (more by arrangement). You can also add a favourite costumed character.

### Areas line (SEO / footer) — приоритетные районы
- *Based in the Los Angeles area — we love performing in **Glendale, Burbank, Pasadena, Santa Monica, Beverly Hills and the San Fernando Valley**, and travel across LA, Orange County and beyond.*
