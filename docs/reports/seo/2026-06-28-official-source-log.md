## (b) Synthesis — what it means for Miss Lana

- **Structured-data eligibility reality check (most consequential):**
  - **Still broadly eligible:** Event (only for *publicly bookable, physical-location* shows), Breadcrumb, VideoObject (≥30s, on a watch page), Image metadata. **LocalBusiness is valid markup** (aids entity understanding) but has **no standalone rich result**.
  - **Restricted:** **FAQPage rich results are limited to authoritative government & health sites since Aug 2023** — Miss Lana will **not** get FAQ rich results. Markup can still aid comprehension; don't expect a SERP enhancement.
  - **Not eligible (self-serving):** Review/AggregateRating star snippets for a business marking up reviews about itself. **Do not add self-hosted Review/rating structured data** — reviews belong on GBP.
- **The site's `CreativeWork` choice for evergreen show pages is correct** — emitting `Event`/`TheaterEvent` without a real `startDate` for evergreen repertoire would violate the structured-data "true representation" policy.
- **Multi-typing `["PerformingGroup","LocalBusiness"]` is valid** and the right entity model.
- **GBP:** textbook service-area business — **hide the address**, define service areas (≤ ~2h drive), pick one specific primary category, never use categories as keywords, never incentivize reviews.
- **Measurement:** use a **GSC Domain property** (covers www/non-www, http/https) + IndexNow + Apple Business Connect; CWV in GSC is **field/CrUX** data (distinct from Lighthouse lab).
- **AI search:** there is **no special "AEO/GEO" lever** (per the May 15 2026 guide) — indexable original content + GBP completeness + reviews are the levers.
- **CWV 2026:** LCP ≤ 2.5s, INP ≤ 200ms, CLS ≤ 0.1 at the 75th percentile of real users; INP (not FID) is the responsiveness metric.
