## Svitlana Grygoryshyna — Director

Owner & director of the theatre. (The old site listed only her name and the

## Armen Tadevosyan — Actor, showman, host & writer

He has starred in the comedy films *Mer Baky 1* and *Mer Baky 2*, the *Comments*
TV show, the *Yere1* sitcom, the *Harevanner* TV series, and the *220V* and
*Kargin Haghordum* comedy series. Today he is a TV host at AMGA TV USA and also
acts in films and commercials and hosts events. His career as a comedian began
in 1993.

## Victoria Stolyarenko — Actress, writer & creative director

A graduate of the Sumy Higher School of Culture and of the Rivne State Humanities
University in Ukraine, where she qualified as a drama-theatre director and
teacher. She has worked in television and cinema.
